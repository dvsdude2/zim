====== **proton vpn commands** ======

===== proton vpn commands =====
__how to use proton vpn [web page]__(https://protonvpn.com/support/official-linux-client/)

- protonvpn-cli login [ProtonVPN username]
- protonvpn-cli connect (or protonvpn-cli c)
- protonvpn-cli disconnect (or protonvpn-cli d)  To see the full list of connection options, enter: 

- protonvpn-cli c --help
